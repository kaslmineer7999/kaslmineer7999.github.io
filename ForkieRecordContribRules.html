COLLABVM FORKIE RECORD CONTRIBUTION RULES

rules for contributing to my forkie record

  1. DO NOT CHANGE THE LOOK OF THE PAGE (E.G. CHANGING BACKGROUND COLOR)
  2. MAKE AN ISSUE FOR PROBLEMS WITH THE PAGE (E.G. TEXT NOT DISPKAYING PROPERLY) AND PR'S FOR ADDING TO THE LIST
  3. GIVE PROOF THAT USER BEING ADDED TO THE LIST IS A FORKIE BY SCREENCAST OR SCREENSHOT
  4. DO NOT SPAM ISSUES OR PR'S FOR ONE PR OR ISSUE
  5. FOLLOW THE ORDER OF OLDEST TO NEWEST RECORD
  6. IF ANY OF THE RULES IS VIOLATED, YOU WILL BE REJECTED
